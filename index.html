Formula basis

EX% = Depot Export / (Depot Export + Comparator Export)
Total Allocation% = (Depot IM + Depot EX) / total IM+EX of both locations.

Calculation basis
Daily / MTD
Daily allocation uses the selected month and stops at the H-1 cut-off. MTD uses cumulative import/export from the first day of that month until each displayed date.
Monthly / Weekly / YTD
Monthly summarizes by calendar month. Weekly summarizes by ISO week. YTD uses cumulative data from 01 January until the selected H-1 cut-off date.
